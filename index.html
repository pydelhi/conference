About the conference

PyDelhi Conference is a premier conference in North India which focuses on using and developing the Python programming language. The conference, now in its first year, will be conducted annually by the PyDelhi community. We hope to attract the best Python programmers from across the country and abroad. The main aim of the conference is to bring together established companies and startups, experts, beginners and global entrepreneurs under one roof. We will be inviting experts from various fields to showcase how Python is used by them and talk about new and upcoming technology.
